Privacy Policy

Overview

Downdraft is designed with your privacy in mind. The app operates entirely on your local machine and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Downdraft does not collect any data. There are no analytics, no tracking, no telemetry, and no crash reporting. The app does not create user accounts or require sign-in.

Network Access

Downdraft does not connect to the internet. All file processing, rendering, and editing happens locally on your Mac. No data ever leaves your device.

File Access

Downdraft only accesses files and folders that you explicitly open through the file picker, drag-and-drop, or the command line tool. The app does not scan your file system or access files outside of your selection.

Third-Party Services

Downdraft does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect data.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact downdraft@knoos.org.
